Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR code?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a type of barcode that can be read by smartphones and QR scanners. It stores information as a series of pixels in a square grid.

What types of QR codes can I create?

You can create QR codes for URLs (website links), plain text, WiFi network credentials (for easy connection), and contact information (vCard format).

How do I scan a QR code?

Most modern smartphones have built-in QR code scanning in their camera apps. Just open your camera and point it at the QR code. If that doesn't work, you can download a QR code scanner app.

Is there a limit to how much data a QR code can store?

QR codes can store up to 4,296 alphanumeric characters or 2,953 bytes of binary data. For most use cases (like URLs or contact info), this is more than enough.
