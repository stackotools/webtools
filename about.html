Our Story

Founded in 2023

With a vision to simplify digital tasks

Simplifying Your Digital Life

StackoTools was founded with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, free online tools that anyone can use without registration or hidden fees.

We noticed that many online tools required sign-ups, had annoying ads, or limited functionality for free users. We wanted to create something better.

Today, StackoTools offers over 100 completely free tools that help with everyday tasks, from PDF conversions to image editing, calculations, and more.
