Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Text to PDF converter really free?

Yes, our Text to PDF converter is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can create as many PDFs as you need without any limitations.

Is there a limit to how much text I can convert?

There's no strict character limit, but very large documents may take longer to process. For best performance, we recommend converting documents under 100,000 characters at a time.

This tool creates standard PDF files that can be edited with any PDF editor. For simple changes, you can edit your original text and create a new PDF.
