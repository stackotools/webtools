Why Use Our Word Counter?

Our free online word counter provides accurate, real-time statistics about your text. Whether you're a writer working on an article, a student crafting an essay, or a social media manager optimizing posts, this tool helps you:

  • Meet word count requirements for assignments and submissions
  • Optimize content length for SEO and readability
  • Track writing progress and set daily goals
  • Analyze keyword density for better content optimization
  • Estimate reading time for better audience engagement

How We Count Words

Our word counter follows standard counting methods:

  • Words: Groups of characters separated by spaces
  • Characters: All visible characters including spaces
  • Characters (no spaces): Only visible characters excluding spaces
  • Sentences: Groups of words ending with punctuation (.!?)
  • Paragraphs: Blocks of text separated by line breaks
  • Reading Time: Based on average reading speed of 200 words per minute
